Mengni Li

My academic interest lies in analysis and nonlinear PDEs. In particular, the long time behavior of the solution to nonlinear wave equations (via commuting vector field method and energy estimates). I am also interested in the global regularity of solutions to Monge-Amp\`ere type equations in convex domains.
